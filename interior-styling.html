Interior Styling

Expert Design Guidance to Transform Your Living Space

Create a Space That Reflects Your Personality

Interior styling is more than just arranging furniture—it's about creating a cohesive, beautiful environment that reflects who you are and how you live. Our expert stylists work closely with you to understand your vision, preferences, and lifestyle needs.

We'll help you make the most of your existing pieces while incorporating carefully selected new items to achieve the perfect balance of style and function. Whether you're starting from scratch or refreshing a single room, we bring creativity, expertise, and attention to detail to every project.

From selecting the perfect color palette to arranging accessories that tie the room together, we handle every aspect of creating a space you'll love coming home to.

What's Included

Comprehensive styling services tailored to your needs

In-Home Consultation

We visit your space to assess the current layout, lighting, and existing pieces while discussing your goals and preferences.

Personalized Design Concept

A custom design plan that includes mood boards, color schemes, and styling recommendations specific to your space.

Space Planning & Layout

Strategic furniture arrangement to maximize flow, function, and visual appeal in your room.

Furniture Recommendations

Expert guidance on selecting new furniture pieces that complement your style and fit your budget.

Accessory Selection

Curated selection of pillows, artwork, rugs, and decorative items to complete your look.

Installation & Styling

Professional placement and arrangement of all elements to bring your design vision to life.

Our Process

From consultation to completion in four simple steps

1

Initial Consultation

We meet at your home to understand your style preferences, functional needs, and budget. We'll assess your space and discuss your vision for the transformation.

2

Design Development

Our team creates a comprehensive design plan including layout options, color palettes, furniture selections, and styling recommendations tailored to your space.

3

Sourcing & Approval

We source all recommended items, present options for your approval, and manage the ordering process to ensure everything arrives on time.

4

Installation & Reveal

We install and arrange all furniture and accessories, adding those finishing touches that make your space feel complete and uniquely yours.
